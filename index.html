This is for the UNITY 2016 Suite! All skinners and themers welcome. Open to Windows, Linux & OS X users. The project is open sourced, we have a chat channel for it on Slack. If you want to come chat with us send me an email at JamieRobertGreen@gmail.com requesting an invite.

UNITY was an open source project created by Jamie Green in 2015.

It's goal was to provide users with a new desktop experience, in the form of Wallpapers, Themes, Icons, Skins and much more! UNITY 2015 turned out to be a huge success with many volunteer contributors helping shape the design of the Suite.

This year we hope for just as big or bigger turnout in respects to the project. With new and old contributors welcome to create a desktop environment that is revolutionary in concept and design. With prospects of it being open to Windows, Linux and OS X users alike. Maybe even contributions to the mobile world this year!

So we look forward in pushing UNITY 2016 out for everyone to enjoy in 2016!

Cheers, Jamie Green (founder)
